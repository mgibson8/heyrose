Membership at Heyrose

As a member of Heyrose you can be as involved as you want to be. We hold weekly competitions for all sections. For those who don't want to take part in these you can come and go as you please, with a organised booking system in operation you more often that not get the tee time you require.

All members have a membership card which must be produced in the pro shop before playing and also can be used behind the bar, where members receive a discount on their drinks.

The pro shop is always able to offer advice, lessons and all the latest equipment. The restaurant provides you with a variety of options all served freshly by our resident chef. Alternatively, relax on the terrace with a chilled glass of wine or pint.

The magnificent course, manicured to the highest standard is inevitability the main attribute of Heyrose and obviously the most important. Our course is considered one of the best in the North West and has been adapted to make it playable in most weather conditions. There is as much to challenge the low handicap golfer as there is to reward the enthusiastic beginner.

For further information on membership or to arrange a look around the club and course please contact us.

Membership Fees

We currently offer, for a limited period, full membership with no entrance fees.

Membership can be paid by standing order over 10 months with an added 4% administration fee. The levy to the English Golf Union and Cheshire County Golf Association, or the Ladies' equivalents, currently £11.25 and £15.00 respectively, will be added to the subscriptions.

All fees are inclusive of VAT.
